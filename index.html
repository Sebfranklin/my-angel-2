He decided to take a shot at this gem. Although it wasn't quite smooth, and she didn't say yes, neither did she say no at first, she had this aura that kept him in a constant state of desire.

He kept pushing forward, loving her and proving his love for the gem.

There are some reasons why he loves her, and one of them is in her name—reasons he is only just discovering.

Her name, Sonia, means wisdom. He was addicted to asking God for wisdom; little did he know it would come in the form of a beautiful damsel. He strives for knowledge, and that is what wisdom is made of.
